Some Cosmological Objects
The CMB (Cosmic Microwave Background) is an imprint of our early universe
Galaxies and gas together give rise to a filament structure in our universe
Black Holes are objects of extreme gravitational strength
Pulsars are incredibly dense, and provide great tests of General Relativity
